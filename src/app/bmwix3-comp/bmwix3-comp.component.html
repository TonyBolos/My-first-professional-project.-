The iX3

The new BMW iX3 looks sportier than ever thanks to the standard M Sport package. Aerodynamic elements and blue design elements remind that this is a fully electric vehicle. Short trips or long trips? With a driving range of up to 456 km* the BMW iX3 is the ideal solution for every trip. * The electric driving range is according to the laboratory tests and may vary depending on the road conditions, climate, vehicle maintenance, driving characteristics and battery wear which affects its capacity and may be lower than the manufacturer's data.
